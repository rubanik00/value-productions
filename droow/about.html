Value Productions

Value Productions - is a company that has already won the trust of customers and continues to improve the skills making outstanding video projects in a big way. Of course, we like challenges and try to explain products in the most exciting way. The company was founded by Peter Garaga in Dnipropetrovsk in 2018..

We work on a lot of video projects, cooperate with different people, and always move beyond. We try to create a video content that will impress not only our clients but also meet our personal preferences.

Each of our projects made to accomplish business and marketing goals. Companies come to work with Value Productions because we understand that video production is an engaging tool for accomplishing marketing goals and furthering the growth of a company.

Primary Services

Explainer videos

We will approach your explainer video from a “beginner’s perspective”, extracting the main essence, keeping things simple, easy to understand, and fun to watch.

Crowdfunding videos

Need to make a splash with your crowdfunding campaign? We have helped our clients raise millions of dollars with our engaging crowdfunding videos.

Video sales letter

Do you want to make a marketing asset that aims to persuade a target audience to purchase a specific product or service? Video sales letter - the best way to do it!

Social media ads

Do you want to use the power of social networks? We can help tell your story on every platform from Facebook to TikTok, and anything that comes next.

E-learning videos

E-learning videos can help enhance the overall learning experience, giving a deeper explanation for certain concepts or providing students with easy-to-follow visuals.

Youtube video productions

One of the best ways to show off your company or product is to inspire your audience with the lifestyle they aspire to join.

Brand videos

Today you have a lot of competitors, use a company brand video to show off your key advantages and why you are the leading on the market.

Product videos

We create product videos that sell. Whether you are selling on your website, Amazon, Shopify in-store, or for a launch, we can help you with a video that drives sales.
